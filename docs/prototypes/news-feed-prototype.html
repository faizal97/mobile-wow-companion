Blizzard Launch March 17, 2026

Midnight Season 1 Has Begun!

By Blizzard Entertainment

The next chapter of World of Warcraft has arrived. Midnight Season 1 brings an all-new raid, eight Mythic+ dungeons, and a wealth of new content to explore.

New Raid: The Scarlet Spire

Face eight new bosses in the Scarlet Spire, the flagship raid of Midnight Season 1. Available in Normal, Heroic, and Mythic difficulties, with the Duskweaver's Regalia tier set dropping from the final four bosses.

"Midnight Season 1 represents our most ambitious season launch yet — we can't wait for players to experience it." — Executive Producer

Mythic+ Season 1 Dungeons

Eight dungeons enter the Mythic+ rotation, including four new Midnight dungeons and four returning favorites:

  • New: Duskhaven Depths, Twilight Bastion, Silvergrove Sanctum, The Crimson Hollow
  • Returning: Mists of Tirna Scithe, Halls of Atonement, Brackenhide Hollow, Uldaman

Class Tuning

  • Retribution Paladin: Final Verdict damage reduced by 8%. Divine Storm increased by 5%.
  • Frost Mage: Glacial Spike damage increased by 12%.
  • Affliction Warlock: Agony reduced by 6%. Drain Soul increased by 10%.

Full patch notes are available on the official World of Warcraft website. Midnight Season 1 is live on all realms now.
